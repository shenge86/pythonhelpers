## Perception on Love #1

A lot of my little fucks are turning into a big one

Fuck my sense of decency and propriety

Nice poems or words in general written by my calm hand

Must be scrubbed clean of f-bombs and s-dynamites

Sometimes I’m fucking tired of this shitty self-censure

I don’t want to appear unrefined so I choose

To hide these vulgar words and vulgar ideas

But how vulgar is it really to express rawness?

I painted my chair a year ago –

a nice little fuck you to someone who I had once loved

She had loved its pure white color and its imitation Parisian style

I never liked inauthenticity; if it’s not French it shouldn’t pretend to be

I painted these words on the back –

*Purity is overrated. This chair can be used for sitting or fucking.*

Done the former plenty of times; haven’t yet tried the latter but I’ll be curious if that little wooden thing can hold two people’s weight.

My mind can be beautifully pure and still have hedonistic desires

I know this when I feel infuriating annoyance at a Christian friend who said to me recently that he’s marrying a girl he dated for 14 months – a paltry amount of time in my perspective

This is his first relationship and he admitted he never had sex; he doesn’t even sleep in the same bed as she this entire time and appeared bashful even stating that he has kissed her

I wish them the best but since I’m a rather jaded soul in some respects I don’t have much faith in a purity like this

Love in what appears to be its purest form can seem beautiful but there’s great risk in giving everything without knowing anything

Knowing nothing about the importance of passion in a relationship

Knowing nothing on what pleasures of the flesh one loves, and to what extent one’s partner is able or willing to provide

Knowing nothing on what the partner desires in bed and what one is able or willing to do

What about me? When I choose to love, I am swept over by passion

I have made love with a handful (nothing I want to openly flaunt since quantity means nothing compared to quality)

Memorable ones are in the woods, in cars (try not to get caught adds to the sexual thrill), on rooftops, in strangers’ beds, etc.

To my Christian friend, if he’s the judgmental type, I must be this hedonistic animal given in to desire

Truth be told, I do tend to have emotions overwhelm me

Perhaps feelings of the moment aren’t really lasting, and physical intimacy may be a mistake, some may argue.

I do not agree. To me, union of spirit to its fullest degree with certainty brings with it a union of the mind and body

Love shouldn’t be segregated from pleasure, and neither are the most apparent in the act… rather, it’s the basking in the afterglow of just laying or sitting there carrying intimate conversation with light touches and sometimes enraptured cuddling

The sensations in life are meant to be fully experienced if the rule of authenticity and respect is present. In other words, be truthful and be kind. There can be beauty and love to the fullest extent inside or outside the societal construct of marriage. Calling it a construct may anger some including this friend but the modern world (or maybe for all human history) has layered rules legal and financial upon that. Its true divine meaning has long lost its importance in light of all this.

I want to not to live in a world bounded by constructs which can bring damage to my emotional and spiritual state. In every society, the original intentions are to offer stability and propriety to humans that cannot be left to chaos and moral decay. For the aim of stability and morality, billions mechanically follow the motions without ever questioning. A lifetime of resigned unhappiness like my parents or a cascade of broken hearts. The rainbow array of human experiences demand that some rules are best left ignored or forgotten.
