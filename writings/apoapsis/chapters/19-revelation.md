# Chapter 19: Revelation

The alarms have gone off and all were quiet. Kara desperately hoped that everyone else were still alive. She still couldn’t move though with her leg jammed between two shelves that had fallen. She could only lie there and talk with whatever this alien was. 

The hologram of the human child stood in front of her staring at her predicament. He/she/it was holding a wand of some kind. The entity smiled and pointed the device at her. “You are the constant, Kara.”

“What do you mean?”

“Think of a set of three coupled differential equations. There is one constant that remains the same in all three differential equations while the other constants are unique to each of the three equations. All three must be solved simultaneously to resolve all of them.”

“And you are saying I’m that single constant which is the same for all three worlds?”

“Worlds can suffice with your language but they’re far more than that. They’re realities which only exist in a bundle. You are that constant which is necessary for the balance of the bundle.”

“Why am I unique?”

“What you humans call fate are just constants in mathematical equations to make the worlds function. Certain people are meant to do certain things at certain times. You are special but you are also not. There are other constants maintaining this balance too, but they have already gone or are not yet here.”

“This problem must be very complicated.”

“Universal balance across multiple shared realities cannot but be complicated. We have simplified the calculations for this particular epoch to only depend on one constant.”

“And that is me? So I am special. What exactly can I do that no one else here can? Everyone here is intelligent and educated and motivated. Some are stronger or smarter than I. What do I have to offer? That’s what I want to know.”

“You are in the ever-present now.”

“You mean I lack the capability to envision the future or reminisce the past? How is that a good thing?”

“Your unique condition in always being in the present emotionally enables you to have the possibility to communicate across shared realities. You just not have yet learned how.”

“Are you here to teach me?”

The entity laughed. It was a light childlike laughter that still seemed too mature coming from this facsimile of a human. The chuckle died away. “You have been learning though you may not know it. Not just your dreams but think of all your interactions on this ship so far. Do you really believe them all to be meaningless coincidences?”

Kara processed the happenings of the past as a list. “I did not see that at the time. So I ask you if this is the end of my lesson.”

“Some say it might be. I say it is only the beginning. Get yourself out of this mess and rescue your crewmates. I will be waiting for you at your next dream.”

The entity started to fade. She had thought that it would have gave her a helping hand in lifting the shelves. Kara frantically cried out. “But wait! I’m still stuck!”

The entity smiled and pointed again at her. Not at her, she realized. She looked down to her right and saw that there was a remote there. It was a remote to move the shelves. She grabbed it and gave a silent prayer that it would work. 

She pressed the button. A motor whirred and the shelf trapping her moved a foot letting her leg loose. She quickly removed her leg from the gap and stood up. She looked around. Everything was a mess.
