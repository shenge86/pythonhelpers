**Dedications**

Most people put down one name. I find it in my heart to be unable to be so restrained. Since I love lists, here is an incomplete list of just a few people in my life that I give thanks to who has helped me grow and become a better person. This book would not be possible without just at least mentioning their names, whether they know it or not.

- Goddess/God for all that She/He created in this beautiful world

- My mom for biologically producing me and her love of literature which by nurture got passed on to me.

- My therapists past and present who were guides on my path towards healing from past traumas and self-understanding.

- Alicia B. who I consider my big sister for always been there for me in both career and emotional support.

- Justin Y. who gave me hope in the future of non-conformist Chinese Americans with near-infinite optimism.

- Megan F. who gave me such love and opened my eyes to what could be possible in literature, artistic expression, and love.

- Richard who considered me his adopted son.

- DJ who understands me on space, science fiction, and life.

- Joe H. who I consider a grandfatherly figure and have offered me continuous emotional support.

- Brett M. who gave me financial advice and emotional support.

- John G. who I work with and pray with.

- Sam W. who I can talk about trajectories in space and emotions.

- Sarah M. who inspires me with both her operational knowledge and her artistic skills.

- Eduardo C. who always provides me spiritual and emotional guidance.

- Michelle F. who inspires me with her courage in tackling the challenges of her own life and her spirit in helping mine. 

- All others who I have yet to mention but know who they are and the outsized roles they have played in my life.
